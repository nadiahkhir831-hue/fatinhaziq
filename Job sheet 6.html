About
G-Dragon, born Kwon Ji-yong on August 18, 1988, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, rapper, and fashion icon. He is the leader of the legendary K-pop group BIGBANG and is widely regarded as one of the most influential figures in Korean pop culture.
Known for his bold fashion choices and innovative music, G-Dragon has released numerous solo albums and collaborations, earning multiple awards and international recognition. He has also been an inspiration for younger K-pop artists and has a strong influence on trends in music and fashion worldwide.
