Our Hotel Stay

The Promenade Hotel Kota Kinabalu offers a comprehensive range of accommodation facilities, comprising a total of 451 well-appointed guest rooms, including Deluxe Rooms, Executive Rooms, and various categories of Suites. These rooms are thoughtfully designed to provide comfort, functionality, and modern amenities, with selected rooms offering panoramic views of the city skyline and the South China Sea. The hotel caters to both leisure and business travelers by combining spacious interiors, quality services, and convenient access to key commercial and tourist areas in Kota Kinabalu.
