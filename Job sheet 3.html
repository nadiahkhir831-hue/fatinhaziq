About

G-Dragon (Kwon Ji-yong) is a South Korean rapper, singer-songwriter, and fashion icon. Known as the "King of K-pop," he rose to fame as the leader of the group BIGBANG.

G-Dragon is a global brand ambassador for Chanel and is considered one of the most influential leaders in music and fashion in Asia.
